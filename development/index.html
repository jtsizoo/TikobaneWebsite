Education, Training, and Capacity Building

We are zealous about educating, training, conveying skills and building the capacity of local upcoming conservationists and other conservationists. The victory of conservation tomorrow depends on the type of active conservation leaders we groom today. Our Training and Capacity building projects are focused on: internships, sports, debates, quiz, and public speaking, schools, conservation education, conservation camps sanctuaries, and national park visits.
