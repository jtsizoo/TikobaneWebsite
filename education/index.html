Gender Equality

Our communities tend to have gender streaming where more attention is given to gender depending on the level of education and cultural background. Thus, we have boys and girls clubs in the communities who are being educated in various topics of benefit. These clubs seem to be beneficial as they boost confidence and self-esteem of kids. Also, we are looking forward to introducing youth, women and men clubs which will equip them to make some commodities in their respective homes. Already the boys and girls club are active (we have a syllabus and weekly activities while others will be implemented in due time). Within our gender equality programs we are focused on the following: Girls Club Boys Club.
